Academic Writing

CALENDAR DESCRIPTION

This course is a guided workshop for students whose programs require the ability to write university-level non-fiction prose. Selected readings help students explore the principles and practice of writing clear and effective sentences, paragraphs, and essays. There will be an introduction to academic research and argumentation. The course is designed to prepare students for university-level scholarship and writing in a variety of disciplines.
